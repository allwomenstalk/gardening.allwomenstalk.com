# DIY Mason Jar Succulents ...

I am the worst at taking care of plants. How about you? Watch this video about how to take care of succulents, the easiest plant there is and how to make unique jars to use for the succulents.

As you probably already know, succulents are like a desert plant, needing to be watered rarely.

As for the jars, in this video, she used fingernail polish and water to decorate. It's a good way to get rid of the nail polish you do not want or need.

There is very little clean-up. Simply put the newly painted jars on parchment paper, then place potting soil in and finally, place the succulents in. Easy, right?

I think these would make cute presents. How about you?

I hope you liked the video.
