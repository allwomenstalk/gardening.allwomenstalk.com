# 5 Signs of Fall I Love ...

Photo Credit: [DMoutray (Denny)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/dennym/4001665489/)

I love the fall. For all of you out there who can’t get over the summer, you need to have a boost to see just why fall is so beautiful. That’s right, I just said that fall is beautiful and it really is. Sure, the sun isn’t shining all the time, but that is one of the things I like. If you need help getting over the summer of 2010, then I want to help you by telling you 5 signs of fall I love …

## 5. The Aroma

I love the aroma in my home every fall. You see, during the fall months, I go out and get candles. These candles may smell like pumpkin pie, pumpkin spice or any of those fall smells. Fall is the only time you will find my home smelling like this.

## 4. The Color in the Home

I love the color in the home during the fall. Of course, I change the colors of my home according to the seasons. During the fall, the curtains go from being bright purple to being orange or black. The couch cover goes to being red. Do you get the picture?

## 3. Pumpkin Pie

Of course, I love the fall because pumpkin pie is one of my favorite things and I don’t really find myself baking a pumpkin pie during the summer months. I save this for one of my fall traditions. Am I weird for this?

## 2. Pumpkins All Together

I like pumpkins all together. I like them big, I like them little, I like them with spooky faces and sitting on my doorstep and windows. I have always had a strange sort of fascination with pumpkins.

## 1. The Weather

I’m not a big sucker for the hot weather and during the summer, it is definitely hot. The fall is near, so that means it is time for the weather to start cooling down and I am very excited.

Those are 5 reasons why I love the fall. If you can think of any other reasons, please tell me!
