# 5 Ways to Get Your Husband to Work in the Garden with You ...

I always think gardening is a rewarding experience for many different reasons. Do you want your husband to work in the garden with you? Prepare for 5 ways to get your husband to work in the garden with you …

## 5. Ask Him

Hey, to start with, before you go on with the other ways to get your husband to work in the garden, why don’t you just ask him? You never know, just asking him might help you out. If he has time on his hand and he’s good man, he’ll help you. However, if he works a lot, he might be tired and may not want to help you.

## 4. Tell Him How It is

You could tell him about the experience that he will get from working in a garden. Tell him about your experience.

## 3. It Relieves Stress

Did you know that working in the garden relieves stress? This is one of the reasons as to why I enjoy working in a garden. Very therapeutic, I think.

## 2. It is Good Exercise

Does your guy think he needs to start exercising? Then tell him how working in a garden is good exercise. It really is. It’s a great way to burn calories.

## 1. You Can Have Fun

I like working in the garden with my husband. I think it is a great way to have fun. Show him just how much fun it can be. It is also a great way to communicate. Ahh, there’s nothing like communicating as you are digging a hole for a garden.

Those are 5 ways to get your husband to work in your garden. Of course, if he refuses to do it, then maybe he just doesn’t want to. Maybe it’s just not his thing and you should not hold it against him. Does your husband work with you in the garden?
