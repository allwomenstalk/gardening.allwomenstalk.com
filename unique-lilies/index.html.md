# 5 Unusual Lilies That Will Make Your Garden Stand Out

I’ve always adored lilies. I never knew there were quite so many different ones until I received a flower catalog with an entire section dedicated to **unusual lilies**. These are 5 **unique lilies** I found among those pages, and I hope you enjoy reading about them. If you're looking for [flower gardening tips](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/flower-gardening-tips-for-beginners/), adding these stunning perennials is a great place to start. I would love to add each of these to my own flower garden someday.

## 5. Adios Nonino Lily

The portion of each petal where they attach at the center of the flower is tinged with a deep purple. About halfway towards the tip of each petal, the purple becomes flecks on the white background and abruptly fades to white. These lilies are some of the first ones to blossom each summer and grow to be around 3 feet tall. For more information on lily care, you can visit the [North American Lily Society](https://www.lilies.org/).

## 4. Miss Lucy Lily

Bright white and spreading to a diameter of 6 inches, these lilies are the world’s first **double Oriental lilies**. They have 24 petals and they are pollenless. They reach a height of 30 to 36 inches. These are an excellent lily to pick for an indoor bouquet being given to someone with severe allergies. Due to the lack of pollen, anyone can enjoy these marvelous lilies. If you love [perennial flowers that bloom all summer](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/perennial-flowers-that-bloom-all-summer/), these are a must-have.

## 3. Anastasia Lily

Have you ever seen a 6-foot-tall lily? This variety reaches a height of up to 6 feet and produces blooms around 7 to 8 inches in diameter. Each hot pink flower emits a spicy-sweet scent as well. The tips of each hot pink petal have a light pink tint and the center where all petals meet is a lime green. These are definitely lilies that will make heads turn. Much like the [Salmon Star Lily](#1), these blooms have beautiful accents that stand out in any landscape.

## 2. Shocking Lily

The contrasting colors of this lily are a real eye-catcher. A red hue graces the center of each golden petal. The red streaks in the center of each petal look more like strokes made with a paint brush. Faint red specks on top of a lighter orange hue can be seen towards the attachment point of each petal. These can reach heights between 36 and 48 inches.

### Lily Variety Quick Guide

| Variety | Height | Color Profile |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Adios Nonino | 3 feet | White & Purple |
| Miss Lucy | 30-36 inches | Double White |
| Anastasia | up to 6 feet | Hot Pink & Green |
| Shocking | 36-48 inches | Gold & Red |
| Salmon Star | 30-35 inches | Salmon & Peach |

## 1. Salmon Star Lily

The white background of each petal is accented with salmon-colored flecks all over the petal. There is also a lighter shade of peach streaking down the center of each petal. These grow to be between 30 and 35 inches in height. You can find more details on various lily cultivars at the [Royal Horticultural Society](https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/lilium).

**Benefits of Growing Unique Lilies:**

- They provide stunning visual impact with minimal effort.
- Many varieties, like the Anastasia, offer incredible fragrance.
- They are excellent for long-lasting cut flower arrangements.
- They attract pollinators like butterflies to your garden.
