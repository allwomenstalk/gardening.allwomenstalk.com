# 8 Essential Lawn Care Tips ...

Lawns are an institution in America. Millions of us have the dream of owning a house with a green lawn and a white picket fence. A healthy, good looking lawn is indeed a matter of pride. Here are some essential lawn care tips to help you care for your lawn in the best possible way and to get the best results:

## 1. Right Grass

Image source: [precisionlawns.biz](https://goo.gl/kV0coZ)

There are many different types of grasses available. It is important that you choose one that is best suited for your region, its climate and the soil. Not only will this make it easier for you to maintain your lawn, the grass will be stronger, healthier and more beautiful.

## 2. Go Organic

More and more people are finding that chemical fertilizers and pesticides do more harm than good to their lawn in the long run. On the other hand, organic substitutes are a healthier and cheaper option.

## 3. Recycle Lawn Clippings

One of the most essential lawn care tips is to leave your lawn clippings on the grass. They will decompose quickly and provide natural fertilizer to your lawn.

## 4. Avoid over Watering

_ Image source: cummingutilities.com_

When there is too much water in the ground, the roots of the grass remain shallow and become susceptible to damage and disease. One of the essential lawn care tips is to ensure that you provide the right amount of water to your lawn. This, of course, will vary from one region to another and one season to another.

## 5. Watering Time

It is highly recommended that you water your lawn early in the morning or late in the afternoon. Watering the lawn during the day results is evaporation of the water and watering it at night can attract bugs and mosquitoes.

## 6. Use a Push Mower

A push mower cuts the grass in a scissor like fashion unlike a gas powered mower which breaks the leaves of grass and damages its roots. You also get to save on the cost of gas and cut down on air and noise pollution.

## 7. Hand Weeding

![Hand Weeding](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ty/pf/dw8rsep854c8f29c76908079719375_500x337.jpg)

Image source: [brucefong.files.wordpress.com](https://brucefong.files.wordpress.com/2008/09/weeding.jpg)

I have found that hand weeding is far more effective that using chemicals to destroy weeds. It is a continuous process but it is better for your lawn.

## 8. Be Environment Friendly

One of the best and essential lawn care tips is to be environment friendly. This is easy to achieve. You can harvest your own rain water for the lawn with the help of rain barrels. You can create your own compost for the lawn with the help of a compost bin or a compost pit in your backyard. These and many other tips like these will help you be friendly to the environment you live in.

By following these essential lawn care tips, you will be able to create a beautiful, healthy and sustainable lawn. Having such a lawn is a great pleasure, whether it is the smell of the freshly mowed grass or the smell of the wet ground when it is being watered.
