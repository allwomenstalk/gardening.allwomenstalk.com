# 24 Things to Grow in Your Garden Even if You Have 0 Green Thumb Genes ...

So you want a lovely yard, but have more of a black thumb than a green one? Not to worry! There are lots of forgiving plants that you can plant in your yard that are hardy and super easy to take care of. Filling your yard with some of these plants means a gorgeous look all summer long, even if you tend to kill plants rather than make them thrive. Things are about to change right now!

## 1. Hosta

![Hosta](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/js/ra/ppr5yi3s55881af8e4210532695694_500x391.jpg)

Source: [8 Low-Maintenance Outdoor Plants for](https://www.curbly.com/low-maintenance-plants)
Hostas add a good touch of green and they attract hummingbirds.

## 2. Aquilegias

![Aquilegias](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/j3/e5j5g0ij55881af96bb79968587086_468x636.jpg)

These flowers are super easy to take care of and they come back year after year.

## 3. Cosmos

![Cosmos](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hh/d7/wy9iqhtc55881af960751711471178_400x400.jpg)

## 4. Lantana

## 5. Hens and Chicks

![Hens and Chicks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/az/hw/yb7qw3so55881afa944b4504376724_600x1634.jpg)

## 6. Peonies

![Peonies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/t3/zdicv6p755881afad9859181543420_600x610.jpg)

## 8. Garden Hydrangea

![Garden Hydrangea](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c4/tx/mvww6x5w55881afa69cba307300633_400x400.jpg)

## 10. Petunias

## 20. Gladiolus
