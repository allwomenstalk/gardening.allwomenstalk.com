# 5 Reasons to Love the Fall ...

I love the fall and there is no denying this. Of course, some people will think I have lost my mind, because they like the summer months more. I am not a big sucker for the sun. I don’t really like the hot weather, so of course, I prefer fall.

## 5. The Changing of Leaves

One of my favorite things about the fall months would be the changing of the leaves. In Florida, it’s beautiful to watch an oak tree’s leaves change to orange and red. I love taking pictures of this.

## 4. The Smell in the Air

You know what I am talking about, right? The smell of pumpkin and all of those lovely spices. The aroma in the air when you walk into stores is beautiful.

## 3. The Excitement

I have to admit, when fall is near, the excitement level rises. At least, for me this is true. I don’t know why, but I am happier during the fall months.

## 2. The Weather is Getting Cooler

I like the fall, because this is when the weather starts to go from hot to cool. The summer in Florida is harsh, so the fall is definitely something to look forward to.

## 1. Halloween

Yes, when I think of the fall, I think of Halloween. Halloween is one of my favorite times of the year.

I think fall is a lovely season. Kids always seem really excited this time of the year. I think it has a lot to do with Halloween and getting to go trick-or-treating. What about you, what is the reasons why you love fall so much? Wait, you don’t like the fall months? Then why do you not like it?
