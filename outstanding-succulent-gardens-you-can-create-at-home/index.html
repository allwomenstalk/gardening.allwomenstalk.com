6. Hens and Chicks

Via Gallery & Inspiration | Picture ...
Those round ones in the front are hens and chicks are a succulent that is virtually effortless to care for.

***

Succulents known as hens and chicks (or by their botanical name, Sempervivum) are delightful little plants that make a charming addition to any succulent garden. These evergreen perennials are valued for their ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions. With a rosette form, the "hen" is the central, larger plant, and the "chicks" are the offshoots that cluster around it. Growing these is a breeze, just provide them with well-draining soil, plenty of sunlight, and occasional water when the soil is completely dry. They're perfect for that no-fuss green touch in your space!
