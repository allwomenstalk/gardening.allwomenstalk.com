# Ways to Display Your Jar Herb Gardens ...

OK. You know how to grow jar herb gardens. But how to display them? Learn **how to display your jar herb gardens** with this video.

The mason jar may be presented in a zillion ways. Painted, with string, with labels, or attached to wood.

Would you like to know which one stood out to me? A simple jar with a clothespin! The name of the herb is written on the clothespin and the clothespin is clipped to the jar. Simple and cute!

You are bound to find a way to display your jar herb garden with this video. The video is full of suggestions.

Dinner is going to taste delicious with herbs grown in your own home.
