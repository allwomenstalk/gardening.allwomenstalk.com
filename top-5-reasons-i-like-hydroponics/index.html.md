# Top 5 Reasons I like Hydroponics ...

I think that everyone out there should try hydroponics. You can keep your normal garden in the yard, but you should also try hydroponics at the same time.

## 5. You Don’t Need Dirt

I like the fact that you do not have to have dirt with hydroponics. No dirt is involved, so now, you can say goodbye to the dirt! Where has hydroponics been hiding all of your life, right?

## 4. You Can do It in a Small Home

Do you live in a small home? Perhaps you live in the city? You probably think that because of this, you cannot grow plants, right? Well, it is time to introduce you to hydroponics. With hydroponics, you will be able to grow plants, even if you live in the city.

## 3. They Get Their Nutrients

When the plants are in the ground, you are not sure if they are getting their nutrients or not. However, when you use hydroponics, then you know for a fact that your plants are getting nutrients and this is something that I really like about hydroponics.

## 2. No Weeds Involved

Come on, do you seriously enjoy pulling up weeds from that outside garden every time you turn around? When you do hydroponics, there is not going to be any need to pull up weeds, because there is no way they can grow in your plants!

## 1. It’s Clean

Because there is no soil or dirt being used with this, growing plants will be clean. Who would have ever thought that you could stay clean and still grow plants at the same time?

Those are the top 5 reasons I like hydroponics. My favorite is because it is clean and no dirt is involved. What about you, why do you like hydroponics so much? Is there anyone who dislikes it and why?
