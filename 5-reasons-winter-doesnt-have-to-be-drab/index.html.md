# 5 Reasons Winter Doesn't Have to Be Drab ...

I have a few friends and acquaintances who dread the thought of winter approaching. I’ve always found plenty to do during the winter. I never seem to be bored or feel that I can’t make it until spring arrives. There are ways to keep busy and to make the winter pass by much quicker. Here are 5 reasons winter doesn’t have to be drab.

## 5. Winter is the Perfect Time of Year to Make Plans for Warmer Weather Activities

What better time to plan vacations, family gatherings, and other outdoor activities than during the winter. You don’t have to worry about missing out on a nice warm sunny day in order to take time out for planning specific events. The winter season will give you plenty of time to work out all the fine details so you are completely ready for warmer weather and all the things you want to do in it.

## 4. There Are Plenty of Winter Sports to Participate in

Skiing, sledding, snowboarding, snowmobiling, and dog sledding all require snow and are excellent activities for the whole family. If you live in an area where the snow doesn’t fall as heavily, then you can still go for bike rides or walks. You might have to bundle up a bit more, but there’s no reason why you should be stuck indoors all winter long.

## 3. Hot Cocoa Parties with Friends Are a Great Excuse for Gathering Together

A cold winter day is always more fun when you can collect a group of friends and invite them over for some thick and creamy hot cocoa. It’s kind of hard to get excited about a hot cocoa party during the summer time.

## 2. You Can Take Time to Watch a Movie and Not Feel Guilty about Staying Indoors

When the weather is nice out during the warmer months, people always seem to wonder why everyone isn’t out enjoying it. By the time winter rolls around and the colder weather approaches, it seems a bit more acceptable to take a day to simply lounge on the couch and eat some popcorn at your leisure.

## 1. Picking up a New Hobby is Easier to do in the Winter Time

I always find plenty to do during the spring and summer, but I find winter is the best time to take up a new indoor hobby. One winter I started sewing quilts and another year during the winter I began learning how to crochet.

The possibilities are endless. There are plenty of things to do so that you don’t get caught up in the winter blues or become bored during the winter time. What ways do you make the winter months pass by faster?
