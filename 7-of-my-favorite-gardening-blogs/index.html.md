# 7 of My Favorite Gardening Blogs ...

I thought I’d share 7 of my favorite gardening blogs that I recently wrote. These are chock full of information to do with not only gardening in general, but also use of herbs, some decorating tips for the garden, and a number of other fun things. You just might find something interesting that you’d like to pass on to others.

## 7. [5 Tips on Trimming Trees](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-tips-on-trimming-trees/)

![5 Tips on Trimming Trees](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/uj/j91hxw0754c8ef12d5593153738675_400x300.jpg)

Making sure you don’t injure your trees or yourself when doing a little tree trimming is a common goal most people have. This short blog gives you 5 tips on how to go about trimming your trees properly. There is a bit more care needed when trimming trees. You can’t simply start whacking branches off left and right or during any time of the year. Take a look at this blog piece to learn some beneficial trimming tips.

## 6. [5 Cool Season Vegetables](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-cool-season-vegetables/)

I love to be in my garden for as much of the year as possible. With the option to grow some cool weather veggies I can still be working in the garden come autumn. I also find it more comfortable to be gardening during the cooler weather. This blog offers descriptions of 5 cool season veggies that are fun to grow and tasty too.

## 5. [5 Tips on Decorating a Garden](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-tips-on-decorating-a-garden/)

![5 Tips on Decorating a Garden](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xn/s3/iqbwlp2i54c8ef1583d32884054030_400x300.jpg)

Maybe after reading these 5 decorating tips for the garden area you’ll be able to come up with a new way to spruce up your garden. Something as simple as adding twinkle lights might work or maybe you want to make it more of a sanctuary for yourself or the local wildlife. This blog will give you at least a few ideas.

## 4. [5 Signs It’s a Good Time of the Year to Plant Citrus Trees](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-signs-its-a-good-time-of-the-year-to-plant-citrus-trees/)

![5 Signs It’s a Good Time of the Year to Plant Citrus Trees](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sm/zt/jmsorjg854c8ef16a5734323620406_400x300.jpg)

I don’t know about you, but I find that citrus trees take a bit more care than other types of trees. If you are new to growing citrus trees and are about to purchase some, then make sure you take a look at this blog. I go over 5 signs that you should look for before planting your citrus trees. You can never be too careful with new trees, especially those of the citrus variety.

## 3. [5 Tips on Preventing Rose Problems](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-tips-on-preventing-rose-problems/)

![5 Tips on Preventing Rose Problems](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/re/jakvnlcg54c8ef17bafe7241485946_400x300.jpg)

I’m surprised at how many problems plague rose plants. Well, at least the domestic varieties have many enemies. This article talks about 5 of the most common problems associated with roses and how to prevent them. I wish they all were as resilient as my wild rose vines and bushes!

## 2. [5 Herbs to Take Caution with](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-herbs-to-take-caution-with/)

![5 Herbs to Take Caution with](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lx/qi/mz0859kj54c8ef18c9da8783911993_400x300.jpg)

I’m all for supplementing my diet with herbs that will help me boost my immune system, lessen the severity of my headaches, or give me tons of energy. However, not everyone is able to consume herbs without having a reaction. Sometimes the reaction is caused by an allergy to the herb, while other types of reactions are caused by the amount of herb that is consumed. I discuss 5 herbs that need to be taken with caution in this blog piece. If you are big on herbal supplements, then you might want to take a peek at this article.

## 1. [5 Healing Herbs](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-healing-herbs/)

![5 Healing Herbs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/sm/mupdby7x54c8ef1a10abe008199548_400x300.jpg)

Herbs aren’t only for cooking; some are beneficial in relieving muscle cramps, headaches, upset stomachs, and any number of ailments. This blog tells you about 5 herbs that are used as natural remedies for different physical problems. There just might be an herb that you didn’t know existed or didn’t know that it had remedial properties!
